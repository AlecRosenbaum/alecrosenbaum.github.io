3-Day Strength & Resilience

Rest: 2–3 min (main)60–90s (accessories) RPE: 7–8 Deload: 6–8 wks
Weekly schedule: Mon (Day 1), Wed (Day 2), Fri (Day 3) — or any ~48h spacing

Standard Prep (start of every session, ~12–15 min)

  1. Jump rope — 3 min (progress to 60–90s continuous bouts)
  2. Mobility Flows
    • Ankle mobility flow — 3 reps in-line, 3 reps perpendicular (just toes) per leg
      • Stand on one leg on a half foam roller.
      • Touch as far as possible: forward, backwards, cross front, cross backwards.
    • Split squat flow — 3×/side
      • Arms above head; feet in line with front foot on half foam roller.
      • Bring hands down and touch knee to the ground (sync with breath).
    • Hip mobility flow — 3×/side
      • Single-leg kneeling; other leg knee bent at 90° pointed out laterally.
      • Toes on half foam roller, heel on ground; hips pointed forward.
      • Extend knee further back to end ROM while maintaining form.
      • Hands up above head, then down on either side of knee (sync with breath).
      • Hands up then down on inside of knee.
  1. McGill Big 3
    • Modified curl-up — 10×10s/side
    • Side plank — 10×10s/side
    • Bird-dog — 10×10s/side
  2. Bretzel — 3×/side

Day 1 — Lower (Squat–Hinge) + Trunk

Power (5–8 min, full rest)

  • Kneeling landmine push press — 3×8 (stop before speed drops)

Strength (35–45 min)

  1. Front squat (elevated heels) — 4×5 @ RPE 7–8
  2. Barbell Romanian deadlift — 3×6–8 @ RPE 7
  3. Front-foot-elevated split squat (goblet) — 3×8/side @ RPE 7
  4. Suitcase carry — 3×30–40m/side

Prehab/Balance (8–12 min)

  • Nordic hamstring (controlled eccentrics) — 2×4–6
  • Pallof press (cable) — 2×10/side (2s hold)

Day 2 — Upper Push/Pull + Rotation Control

Power (5–8 min)

  • Cable “speed press” (split-stance; light load, explosive concentric) — 5×3

Strength (35–45 min)

  1. Barbell bench press — 4×5–6 @ RPE 7–8
  2. Military press (strict OHP) — 2×6–10 @ RPE 7
  3. 1-arm cable row — 3×8/side @ RPE 7 (pause + full reach)
  4. Pull ups — 3×AMRAP @ RPE 7–8

Prehab/Rotation (8–12 min)

  • Copenhagen adduction — 2×8–12/side
  • Cable external rotation @ ~90° + face-pulls — 2×12–15 each

Day 3 — Posterior Chain + Unilateral/Rotation

Power (5–8 min)

  • Landmine rotational clean-to-press — 3×3/side (full rest 60–90s)

Strength (35–45 min)

  1. Conventional deadlift (from floor or 2″ blocks) — 4×3–5 @ RPE 7–8
  2. Walking lunge (plates held at sides or chest) — 3×8/leg @ RPE 7
  3. Seated cable row — 3×8–10 @ RPE 7
  4. Cable chop or cable lift — 2–3×10/side

Prehab/Balance (8–12 min)

  • Nordic hamstring (lighter) — 2×3–5
  • Single-leg RDL (bodyweight reach or light plate reach) — 2×8/side (slow)
